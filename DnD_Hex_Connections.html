Six Questions to Ask

1. Who is here? (People)
2. What is valuable here? (Resource)
3. Who wants it? (Needs, Trade, Power)
4. Who knows something about it? (Knowledge, Secrets)
5. Who has been affected by it? (History, Conflict)
6. Where does it point next? (A lead to another location or adventure.)
