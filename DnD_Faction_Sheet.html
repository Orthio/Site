  • Tier: The rating for a faction
  • Quality: For a gang this is the same Tier rating number

  • Magnitude: For supernatural events
        1d for each Trait Rating
        +1d for each Major Advantage
        -1d for each Major Disadvantage

        Critical: Exceptional Result (Roll more than one 6)
        6: Good Result/Standard, Full Effect
        4-5: Mixed Result/Limited, Partial Effect
        1-3: Bad Result/Poor, Limited Effect
